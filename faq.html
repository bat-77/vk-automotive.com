Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before booking your premium detail with VK Automotive.

How long does a detailing session take? +
The duration depends on the package selected and the condition of your vehicle. As a general guide:

Standard Interior or Exterior — typically 1.5 to 2.5 hours.
Standard Combo — approximately 3 to 4 hours.
Premium Interior or Exterior — typically 3 to 5 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.
Premium Combo — can take a full day (6–8 hours) for exceptional results.

Vehicles that haven't been detailed recently, or those with particularly heavy soiling, may require additional time. KV will always give you an honest estimate before work begins.
Where does the service take place? Do you come to me? +
Yes — VK Automotive operates as a fully mobile service. We come to your location, whether that's your home, workplace, or any other accessible location in Limburg and the surrounding areas.

All you need to provide is access to the vehicle and a reasonably sheltered or covered space (a garage, carport, or even a quiet street works fine). We bring all our own professional-grade equipment and products.

If you're unsure whether your location is within our service zone, don't hesitate to get in touch — we're flexible and always happy to discuss options.
How does payment work? What methods do you accept? +
Payment is due upon completion of the service, once you've had a chance to inspect the results and are fully satisfied. We accept:

Cash — always accepted.
Bank Transfer (Virement) — details provided at booking confirmation.
Bancontact / Payconiq — available on request.

There are no hidden fees. The price quoted at booking is the price you pay — nothing more. Deposits may be required for Premium Combo bookings or new clients; this will be communicated clearly during confirmation.
Do you detail custom, exotic, or high-value vehicles? +
Absolutely — and it's something we particularly enjoy. VK Automotive has experience working on a wide spectrum of vehicles, from everyday hatchbacks to high-performance sports cars, luxury SUVs, AMGs, Porsches, and exotic marques.

With premium and exotic vehicles, we apply extra care at every stage: pH-neutral products, dedicated microfibre cloths, safe washing techniques, and hand-applied protection. If you drive something special, you can trust that it will be treated accordingly.

For particularly rare or high-value vehicles, we recommend booking a consultation call so we can discuss your vehicle's specific requirements before the appointment.
When will the luxury rental service launch? +
The VK Automotive Luxury Rental service is currently in active development and is expected to launch in the near future. We are in the process of curating the fleet, establishing logistics, and ensuring that every rental vehicle meets the exact same standard we apply to our detailing work — which means immaculate.

To be notified as soon as rentals go live, we recommend following our social media channels or contacting us directly to register your interest. Early registrants may benefit from priority access and exclusive launch pricing.
What if I'm not satisfied with the result? +
Client satisfaction is the entire point. If you're not happy with any aspect of the work, tell us before we leave and we will address it immediately — no argument, no hesitation.

Our goal is for every client to look at their vehicle when we're done and feel genuinely impressed. The VK Automotive reputation has been built on real results, and we have every intention of keeping it that way.
Will the diagnostics and repair service be available soon? +
Our diagnostics and repair branch is in the planning phase. It is being developed with the same commitment to quality that defines our detailing service — which means we will only launch it when we are confident it can be delivered to the standard VK Automotive clients expect.

In the meantime, if you have a repair or diagnostic need and would like a personal recommendation for a trusted mechanic in the Limburg area, don't hesitate to contact us. We're always happy to help point you in the right direction.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 48 to 72 hours in advance to secure your preferred date and time slot. Weekends and holiday periods tend to fill up quickly, so earlier booking is always advisable if you have a specific date in mind.

For same-day or next-day requests, we do our best to accommodate when availability allows — contact us directly via phone or WhatsApp at +32 468 47 45 69 for the fastest response.
